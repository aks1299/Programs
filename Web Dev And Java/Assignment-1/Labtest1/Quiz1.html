About:

ITER Bhubaneswar has a total of 7 Engineering departments for B.Tech under the umbrella of its undergraduate programs and also offers other lateral courses in the field of engineering. Whereas for the PG level, the institute offers M.Tech, the college also offers various other courses MBBS, BCA, MBA and MDS. In addition to this, the University also offers Ph.D. courses in this certain subject.






Departments at ITER :
  1. Civil Engineering
  2. Computer Science and Engineering
  3. Electrical Engineering
  4. Mechanical Engineering
  5. Electronics and Communication Engineering
